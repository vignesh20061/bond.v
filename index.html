What are Bonds?

Bonds are fixed-income financial instruments where investors lend money to an issuer in exchange for regular interest payments and repayment of principal at maturity.

Indian Bond Market

The Indian bond market plays an important role in the country’s financial system. It helps the government and companies raise funds for development and expansion.

Why Invest in Bonds?

  • Stable and regular income
  • Lower risk compared to stocks
  • Capital protection
  • Diversification of portfolio
