What are Corporate Bonds?

Corporate bonds are issued by companies to raise capital for business operations, expansion, or debt refinancing.

Types of Corporate Bonds

  • Secured Bonds
  • Unsecured Bonds
  • Convertible Bonds
  • Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs)

Risk and Return

Corporate bonds usually offer higher returns than government bonds but carry higher risk depending on the company’s credit rating.
