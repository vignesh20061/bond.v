What are Government Bonds?

Government bonds are debt instruments issued by the Government of India or State Governments to raise funds for public spending.

Types of Government Bonds

  • Government Securities (G-Secs)
  • Treasury Bills (T-Bills)
  • Savings Bonds
  • State Development Loans (SDLs)

Advantages

Government bonds are considered very safe as they are backed by the government. They are ideal for risk-averse investors.
